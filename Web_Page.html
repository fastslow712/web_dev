Skyward Sword

History

Long ago Demize tried to seize the triforce. He was imprisoned and his army was scattered. In the effort the humans, Gorons, and Kikwes were almost destroyed. The Gorons and Kikwes became wanderers. What was left of the humans lived in a village on an island in the sky named Skyloft. With the imprisonment of Demise the war ended as his army was scattered as where the Gorons and Kikwes and the humans could not get off Skyloft. Even with the war over, the Gorons and Kikwes still had an occasional scuffle so their peace was not complete. Even so, the semi-peace lasted for hundreds of years.

Start of the adventure

Hundreds of years after the end of the war, the humans, now called the Skyloftens, were still living in peace in Skyloft. Despite the fact that they were living in complete peace, not the semi-peace of the Gorons and Kikwes, they still had a military force even if it was small. The soldiers for this force were trained at the knight academy of Syloft. The students at this academy were divided into two groups, senator and junior. Every year a competition was held. In this competition the junior students competed and the winner moved up to being a senior student. After one of these compositions the headmaster's daughter, Zelda, was pulled down to the surface by a flying black tornado. Then that year's winner, Link, went down to find her.
